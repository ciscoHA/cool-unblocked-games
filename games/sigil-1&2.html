Instructions and Overview

How to Play Online (with about 2 seconds of ping)
1. Select ipx on the death manager screen (down arrow and enter)
2. Navigate to the left sidebar menu then choose a room code and turn ipx on
3. Press f10 or fn+f10 to start

Relentless demons, impossibly destructive guns, and fast, fluid movement provide the foundation for intense, first-person combat - Play the game that started the first person shooter genre.
